My Internship Experiences

Learn about the internships I've had and my work at each company.

Internship at Tesla

During my internship at Tesla, I worked on the auto-pilot car computer electronics, focusing heavily on failure analysis. I was responsible for identifying and resolving issues within the car’s electronic systems, which were critical to ensuring the reliability of the self-driving features. I gained valuable experience in both hardware and software aspects of automotive electronics, working with cutting-edge technology.

Internship at Milwaukee Tool

My second internship was at Milwaukee Tool, where I worked on the design and improvement of battery chargers for M12 and M18 battery packs. I was part of a team that aimed to enhance charging efficiency, safety, and battery life. My role involved testing and troubleshooting the charger circuits, and I gained a deeper understanding of power electronics and battery management systems.

Involvement in Wisconsin Racing FSAE

In addition to my internships, I am actively involved with Wisconsin Racing FSAE, a student organization dedicated to designing and building electric vehicles for Formula SAE competitions. As an electronics engineer on the team, I work on the vehicle’s electronic systems, including the motor controllers, battery management, and other critical components. This experience has given me hands-on knowledge of electric vehicle technology and team-based engineering problem-solving.
